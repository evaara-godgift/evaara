Evaara Initiative

About Us

The name of our organisation - Evaara, is derived from the Sanskrit word Evara meaning God's gift. Like the name, we believe that children are the gifts of God. And we believe that every child has the right to smile and shine despite their conditions and abilities. So here, at Evaara, we aim to help and empower underprivileged children. By spreading awareness and organizing various engaging activities and programs, we believe that we can make a change in the lives of these children. We strive to spread joy, build confidence and shape a world where each child is celebrated and known for who he/she is.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower underprivileged children. Like our name Evaara, meaning God's gift. We believe that every child, being a gift of god, deserves to smile and shine. We strive to build confidence in each child and bring out the best in them. We strive to connect these little bravehearts and help them grow.

Our Vision

Our vision is very simple. We wish to see a society where each child is celebrated for who he/she is. We wish to empower all these little children by helping them improve their skills and brighten their lives. We wish to make a change in the society, fostering inclusivity and happiness.
